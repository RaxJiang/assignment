WHO WE ARE

PrimeAsia manufactures sustainable and innovative leathers for the global branded footwear, apparel, and leather goods markets. PrimeAsia has two manufacturing sites located in Dong Guan, China and Ba Ria-Vung Tau, Vietnam. These manufacturing sites are supported by a global sales and marketing team with offices in the USA (East and West coasts), UK as well as a broad network of sales and support staff in regional Asia.
